Frequently Asked Questions

There's always a new challenge. We always learn something new. But let's try to get ahead of ourselves.

  • Can I pay my lessons and gear with a credit card?

    Yes, we take all major credit cards.

  • What happens if conditions aren't optimal for my lesson?

    Lessons will only be done on optimal conditions. If this is not the case, you can always reschedule.

  • Can I bring company while I kite?

    Yes, the beach club is included for the kiters company so they can hang out while you kite.

  • What should I bring & wear to my Kite or Wing Class?

    Bring your swim cloth and a lot of sun protection (sunscreen, sunglasses, hat).

  • Can I pay my lessons and gear with a credit card? How can I pay my lessons, accommodation, or restaurant bill?

    You can pay with credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and cash.

  • Can I bring my own food & beverages?

    As we have our own restaurant, there is no need to bring your own food & beverages. If you still want to bring something, you can consume it on the beach or in your private cabin.

  • Can everyone take kite or wing lessons?

    Adults or kids, men or women, anyone can learn. With today's technological advances in design, the sport has never been safer. Under the correct guidance, you can be up and going in no time.

  • Can I park my car at the spot?

    We have a huge parking lot where you can station your car for free for the day.

  • How can I reach Punta Chame, Panama?

    You can either come with your own car (approx. 1.5 hours from Panama City), take a local bus, or a transfer. Just get in contact with us, and we can help you with your travel planning.
